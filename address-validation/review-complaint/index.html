Finalize submission

You will not be able to attach documents, edit this complaint, or reopen this complaint after you submit.

The company will use the information in your complaint to respond to you, so please make sure everything is accurate and complete before continuing.

What will happen when you submit your complaint

  1. The CFPB will send your complaint directly to the company unless we can’t get in touch with the company, it is not set up respond to complaints, or if another agency is better able to assist. The CFPB will not read your complaint before sending it to the company.
  2. The company will respond directly to you. The company will review the issues you have described in your complaint and any supporting documents you have attached. Companies generally respond in 15 days. In some cases, the company will let you know their response is in progress and provide a final response in 60 days.
  3. The CFPB will ask you for feedback. You’ll be able to review the company’s response and will have 60 days to provide feedback about the company's response. Your feedback about how the company responded to your complaint is important because CFPB can't change a complaint outcome and you can't resubmit about the same issue. Your feedback also helps improve the complaint process for everyone.
